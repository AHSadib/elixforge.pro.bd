Privacy Policy

Radical transparency about how we handle your data.

Last updated: January 2025
1. Information We Collect

ElixForge apps collect only the minimum data required: account info (email for sign-in), app usage data (habits, logs, streaks), and device info for crash reporting. We do NOT collect location data, contacts, or any sensitive personal information beyond what functionality requires.

2. How We Use Your Data
  • To sync your habits across devices via cloud backup
  • To send habit reminders you have configured
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs (anonymized)
  • To display relevant ads via Google AdMob
3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption. Your habit data is private and accessible only to you.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our apps are free and supported by optional rewarded ads. You choose when to watch an ad — ads are never forced. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use: Google Firebase (auth, database, analytics), Google AdMob (advertising), and Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, delete, and export all your data at any time. Contact: adib85249@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: adib85249@gmail.com
